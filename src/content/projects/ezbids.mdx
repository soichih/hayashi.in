ezBIDS is a web tool that converts neuroimaging data and metadata into the **Brain Imaging Data
Structure (BIDS)** standard — with no installation or programming required.

It handles both imaging and task-events data, offers semi-automated inference and guidance so
datasets adhere to BIDS, and supports multiple data-management options including direct transfer
to OpenNeuro.org or brainlife.io. It removes one of the most tedious, error-prone steps in the
neuroimaging workflow, making standardized data far more accessible to labs.

- **Published:** Levitas, D., Hayashi, S. et al. "ezBIDS: guided standardization of neuroimaging
  data interoperable with major data archives and platforms." *Scientific Data* 11, 179 (2024).
  [doi:10.1038/s41597-024-02959-0](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-024-02959-0)
- **Try it:** [brainlife.io/ezbids](https://brainlife.io/ezbids)
